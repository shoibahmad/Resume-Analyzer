How ResuMind AI Works

1

Identity & Context Handshake

Every analysis starts with a secure handshake. Firebase handles identity management while our backend initializes a dedicated session instance to track your resume's versioning history in Firestore.

2

Multi-Modal Ingestion

We don't just "read" your resume; we ingest it. Our parsing engine uses PyMuPDF to extract text and rasterize visual layouts simultaneously, ensuring that both your technical content and your visual design are preserved for analysis.

3

AI Logic Synthesis

Using the Gemini 2.5 Flash engine, we perform a hybrid synthesis. We compare your skill density against 50,000+ industry-standard job benchmarks, calculating ATS compatibility and professional tone strength in milliseconds.

4

Shadow Recruiter Vision Audit

This is where computer vision takes over. The AI looks at your resume just like a human eye would. It evaluates white space, visual hierarchy, and "eye-fatigue" metrics, providing 'brutal' but actionable design feedback.

5

Predictive Dashboarding

Finally, all raw data points are transformed into a dynamic dashboard. We generate a 10-year career blueprint, salary negotiation tactics, and high-impact rewrites of your weakest bullet points.

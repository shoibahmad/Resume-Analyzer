Privacy Policy

At ResuMind AI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data and the steps we take to protect your confidential information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect the content of the resumes you upload and the job descriptions you paste. This information is used solely for the purpose of performing the requested AI analysis.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Analysis: We process your resume text through Large Learning Model to generate insights.
  • Improvement: We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve the accuracy of our matching algorithms.
  • No Resale: We never sell your personal contact information or resume data to third parties.

3. Document Storage

ResuMind AI is designed with a "Privacy First" mindset. In our open-source version, uploaded documents are processed in memory and not permanently stored on our servers unless specifically requested by a feature (such as Scan History).

4. AI Infrastructure

Your data is processed using Google's generative AI infrastructure. By using the service, you agree to Google's standard data processing terms for AI interactions. We recommend removing highly sensitive personal details (such as SSNs or residential addresses) from resumes before upload.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption for all data transfers, to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you or to request the deletion of your scan history at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced on our homepage.
